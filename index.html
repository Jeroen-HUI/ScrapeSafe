Everything you need to drive slammed.

ScrapeSafe gives you the tools to navigate safely, contribute to the community, and connect with other people who daily their slammed builds.

01

Smart Routing

See obstacles on your route as you navigate. Free users can view all community-reported speed bumps, steep driveways, scrapy parking garage entrances, and problem areas. Pro members get automatic rerouting that steers you away from frame-scraping obstacles before you reach them. Routes adapt in real-time based on your car's ground clearance and live community data.

02

Clearance Scanner

Point your camera at any slope, curb, or obstacle. Enter your car's ride height. ScrapeSafe calculates the angle and tells you if you'll make it. No more guessing. No more scraping. Just point and know. Available for Pro members.

03

Community Reports

Every driver contributes. Spot a speed bump that'll scrape your splitter? Mark it. Find a safe route through a cambered road? Share it. Know which gas stations have steep entrance angles? Report them. The more people contribute, the better the data gets for everyone running low. You help the community. The community helps you.

04

Build Showcase

Post photos of your slammed car. Share your wheel setup, offset specs, suspension mods, and how low you're running. See what other people in the community are on. Get inspired. Show off what makes your static or bagged build unique.

05

Meet Hosting

Organize stance meets, static sessions, or bag shows on the map. A small hosting fee keeps spam away and ensures only real events get posted. Discover meets near you. Connect with people who actually care about fitment, wheel gap, and tucking tire.

06

Real-Time Alerts

Get notifications about new obstacles on your saved routes, construction that affects clearance, or changes reported by the community. Stay informed. Drive prepared.
